Stewie Griffin

Stewie is the male human child of Brian Griffin andchr, voiced by actor Seth MacFarlane.

He was born in 2003. He has two sisters, Chrissy and Meg, who are not related to him biologically.

Stewie's intellect, along with his malice and vindictiveness, often gets him into trouble.
